About

This project is a submission for the NASA INTERNATIONAL SPACE APPS CHALLENGE held in the duration of OCTOBER 1-2, 2022.

The Challenge

When they explored the Moon, NASAâ€™s Apollo astronauts left behind several instruments to collect geophysical data near each Apollo landing site. Your challenge is to develop an app that plots the seismic data these instruments transmitted back to Earth on an interactive 3-D globe.

Working

This project makes use of the data provided by the NASA SPACE APP. The seismic points present on the moon got from this data were plotted on a 3D moon replica. All points have been denoted by the geographical coordinates of the moon. When we hover over these points, these coordinates are shown for the ease of the user. The date of the data collected is also shown.

Mission

This project is open-source, allowing us to provide this information to the open community for various research and academics. This representation can prove to be useful for these requirements.
